2. Fungsi dan Keuntungan dari Menggunakan VPN#

Using a Virtual Private Network (VPN) is beneficial for many reasons. A VPN establishes a secure connection between two points, allowing users to safely access websites and services without worrying about data being leaked. Since a VPN encrypts data, it also ensures that all of a user’s online activities remain protected and private from spying eyes. Additionally, VPNs allow users to access geo-restricted websites, allowing access to more content when travelling abroad. Finally, a VPN prevents ISP throttling, ensuring that all of a user’s data is being transmitted without any slowdowns. In conclusion, a VPN provides an invaluable service in the digital world by providing a secure connection and allowing users to access content and services that would otherwise be inaccessible.
