First, open Google Chrome and click on the three vertical dots in the top-right corner. From the drop-down menu, select the “Settings” option. Under the “Advanced” section, click on “Network” and then select the “Add Private Network” option.

Next, enter the IP address of the VPN server you want to connect to, the port number, and the username and password. You can find all of this information on the VPN provider’s website. Once you’ve entered the information, click on “Save” to save the connection.

Now, you’re ready to connect to the VPN server. Go back to the three vertical dots in the top-right corner and select the “Connect” option. Once you’ve connected, the VPN icon should appear in the top-right corner of Chrome.

Finally, you should check to make sure the VPN is working correctly. Visit a website that shows your IP address and you should see that it has changed to the IP address of the VPN server. You can also do an internet speed test to make sure your connection is secure and fast.
