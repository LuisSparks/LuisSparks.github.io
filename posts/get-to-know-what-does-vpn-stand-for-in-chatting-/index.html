1. Explain What is VPN#

A Virtual Private Network (VPN) is a technology that enables users to securely access a private network such as the internet. It does this by allowing users to securely connect to a remote server and mask their internet activities from third parties. A VPN encrypts the traffic that is sent between the user’s device and the VPN server, making it a secure connection. This is particularly useful for accessing geo-restricted websites or content, or for securing data when browsing online. Additionally, VPNs can be used to protect user data while using public Wi-Fi networks. In every way, VPNs are a great way to maintain online privacy and security.
