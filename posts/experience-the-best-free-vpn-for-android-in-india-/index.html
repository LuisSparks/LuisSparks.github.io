Q1. What is a VPN?

A1. A VPN (virtual private network) is a secure connection between two or more devices, allowing for private and encrypted internet access to protect your data and browsing activity from being seen by the outside world. It does this by routing all of your internet traffic through a secure server, which then encrypts it. This ensures that your data and activity remain secure and private.
